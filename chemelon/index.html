Outsourcing can provide many benefits to companies, including cost savings, access to a global talent pool, improved efficiency, flexibility, risk management, and time savings. Flexibility

Outsourcing can help a company manage risks by transferring certain tasks or activities to a third party that has specialized skills and expertise in that area.
